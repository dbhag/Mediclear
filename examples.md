# Medical Text Simplification Examples

1. Patients with hypertension should reduce sodium intake.
   People with high blood pressure should reduce salt intake.

2. The patient is experiencing dyspnea during mild exertion.
   The person has shortness of breath with light activity.

3. Administer the medication orally twice daily after meals.
   Take the medicine by mouth two times a day after eating.

4. Individuals with diabetes mellitus should monitor blood glucose regularly.
   People with diabetes should check their blood sugar often.

5. The lesion was determined to be benign after histopathological examination.
   The tissue was found to be non-cancerous after lab testing.

6. Discontinue use immediately if an allergic reaction occurs.
   Stop using it right away if you have an allergic reaction.

7. Persistent edema may indicate underlying cardiac dysfunction.
   Ongoing swelling may be a sign of a heart problem.

8. Patients undergoing chemotherapy may experience nausea and fatigue.
   People getting chemotherapy may feel sick to their stomach and very tired.

9. Maintain adequate hydration to prevent renal complications.
   Drink enough water to help avoid kidney problems.

10. Topical corticosteroids can reduce inflammation and pruritus.
    Steroid creams can lessen swelling and itching.
