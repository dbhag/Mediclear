# Simplifier Demo Examples

1. Patients with hypertension should reduce sodium intake.
   People with high blood pressure should reduce salt intake.

2. Myocardial infarction requires immediate medical attention.
   A heart attack needs medical help right away.

3. Metastatic disease indicates the cancer has spread.
   Metastatic disease means the cancer has spread to other parts of the body.

4. The patient is experiencing dyspnea during mild exertion.
   The person gets short of breath with light activity.

5. Topical corticosteroids can reduce inflammation and pruritus.
   Steroid creams can reduce swelling and itching.
